Acuva Brand Book

Graphic Design

Overview

Acuva Brand Book is a brand book project, where we chose the water filtering company, Acuva, and rebranded it.

Context, Skills, and Tools

  • Tools: Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop
  • Type: Academic project (IAT 102)
  • Date: Mar–Apr 2023
  • Team: Sam (myself)
  • Role: Graphic Designer

Problem or Objective

The objectives of this project was to make a logotype that represents the values of the company, Acuva, which is a company that creates technology to purify water. Along with this, we created a Brand Book, showing the logotype on products, letterheads and business cards, along with logotype variation​s, and brochures.

Process

    Logotype Ideation

  • Created a Mind Map to get ideas on what to convey with the logotype, and what the company does

  • Drew different logotypes that could convey the company's values

    Refined Version

  • Sketched layouts for business cards and letterheads.

    Logotype Variations

  • Chose colour schemes based on different color wheels to match Acuva's themes of water and technology

    Applying to Logotype

  • Applied these colors as individual layers to create each logotype, using InDesign

Final Outcome

The result of this project was a branding book, containing relevant information about the company. Along with it, was the process for the creation of the deliverables, such as the logotype, business cards, and letterhead.

Takeaways

This project provided insight into the process of branding design. There was an emphasis on ideation through methods such as mind maps, matrices, and drawing.

In terms of technical skills, I learned how to create layouts and logos using InDesign and Illustrator, as well as kerning for my logotype.

Finally, this project taught of the importance of user research. Researching the company's goals and values were necessary because they informed my design decisions. Also, this is necessary so that your design can reflect the identity of the company.
