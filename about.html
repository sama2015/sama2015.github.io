Who I Am

My name is Sam Aminjavaheri, and my area of focus is UX/UI Design. I aim to create layouts and interfaces that are appealing and are clear for users to understand and navigate. I do this by making mockups on Figma and testing different fonts, colour palettes, and styles for necessary elements and how they complement each other or not.

Skills

Skills that I have that are relevant in my field are making wireframes, mockups and prototypes in Figma, creating responsive designs, and designing interfaces that are appealing to users and suit their needs.
